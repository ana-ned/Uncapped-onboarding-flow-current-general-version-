This tool models daily fee accrual and balance-based advances for illustration purposes only. Actual advances, fees, and repayments are governed by your Master Receivables Purchase Agreement and the applicable Offer. Daily Fees accrue on the Outstanding Advance Amount and are not compounded. When a payout reduces the total balance below the current advance, a repayment is triggered on the same day for accrued fees plus the principal difference. Advances over weekends may land on the next business day. Deferred balance release timing is approximate; actual release schedules vary by marketplace.
